Best Restaurants in Pangasinan

Matutina's Gerry's Seafood Restaurant, Urdaneta

Matutinas is well-known not only in Pangasinan but also in other provinces in the North such as Pampanga and Tarlac. So famous is the chain of restaurants that their original branch in the seaside village of Tondaligan (also within Pangasinan) has been visited by notable personalities like the Pope John Paul II and former Philippine president Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo.

Jech’s Bakeshop and Restaurant, Calasiao

Jech’s is popular for its take home and pasalubong bakery section as well as the restaurant. They serve home cooked style dishes as well as seafood and a variety of other meals

Dampa's Seafood Restaurant, Urdaneta

The best seafood resto in the north. If you are fond of pinoy food, try their bangus inihaw and sinigang na malaga, you will love them with the bagoong. Crispy pata and pinakbet are the best, you will crave for more. If you are a first-timer, you will surely go for a repeat. People are willing to wait, after-all, the food are superb, service is okay and the ambiance is awesome and clean.
